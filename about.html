About Us


We are UniThirstQ (University Thirsty Quencher) which aims at helping students in the University of Sydney to find the most desirable way to quench their thirst. No matter you are students, staffs or even visitors, please feel free to register in our system. You can look for all drinking location in the University and search the one you like based on the criteria you choose, rate and review on the drinking facility and even more feauture! Join our system ASAP!
