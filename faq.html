Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I have to register an account to use the system?
No, you don't need to. You can search, sort and browse all drinking locations without an account. But registering an accout is highly recommended since you could add your favourites and leave reviews with an account.
2. I found one of the locations on the list is having some issue, what should I do?
Please click the "report issue" button underneath the info page of each location. This will bring up a form for you to fill in. The management team will address the issue within 1 business day
